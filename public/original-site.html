What's Inside

Materials that
earn their place

◈

Pocketed Steel Coils

Tempered steel springs, individually fabric-pocketed to eliminate partner disturbance. Precision-calibrated tension ensures longevity without sagging.

❄

Gel Memory Foam

Open-cell gel foam with phase-change technology dissipates body heat, maintaining an optimal sleeping temperature through the night.

◎

Knitted Fabric Cover

Breathable four-way stretch knit, naturally moisture-wicking and hypoallergenic. Quilted for a plush hand-feel that's both durable and supremely soft.

▣

High-Density Base

A 25mm high-density polyurethane base anchors the entire construction, preventing foundation flex and giving the coil system a stable, unyielding platform.
